About Me
Press
Sam grew up in the US but has lived in Asia since 2006.

Photographer

Sam began as a recreational travel photographer but became the Head of Photography for 2 years at Uber Technologies. His work focuses on the different lifestyles of people around the world and highlights our shared humanity.

Developer

After minoring in Computer Science at Stanford, Sam got back into app development nearly 15 years later. He focuses on the entire stack, loving data analytics as well as front-end design.

Investor

Sam manages a small investment fund called Faster Horse Ventures which focuses on a mix of venture capital, real estate, and emerging market opportunities.
